Why Digna

Because no one should have to renounce their dignity for money.Digna (DGN) is a coin designed to align financial value with human value.Every step we take has a simple goal: to enable helping without humiliating, supporting without dominating, sharing without losing.Digna V2 continues this mission with a smarter contract and stronger transparency.

How does it work?

On every transaction, 5% is automatically allocated:2% redistributed to all DGN holders (your balance grows).2% to the Solidarity Fund (public wallet) to finance food baskets, micro-loans, and social projects.1% funds the team (salary, operations, development).
